Ergonomics

What is Ergonomics?

Ergonomics is the study of the relationship between the human workers and their environment

What is the goal?

The goal is to create a total environment that caters to the physical, emotional and psychological aspects of the workplace experience

Information about Ergonomics

Ergonomics is the science of designing equipment for human use by considering human physical and psychological needs and work requirements. Ergonomically designed equipment involves the comfort, satisfaction, productivity and health of employees.

Why do people use computers?

Because they makes everyone's lives much more easier than writing down with pen and paper, but that being said there are some problems associated with them as well like sore eyes, headaches, sore backs, painful wrists and other things by using them for excessive time. Ergonomists deal with these sort of things and change to environment of the workplace to make sure these above injuries are prevented to the best of their abilities.
